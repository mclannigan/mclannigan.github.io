SOARbucks Startup

Project— Staley High School will design, plan, and build a functioning student-run coffee shop that can support itself.

Purpose— A coffee shop would create a culture of belonging and great coffee.  Students have been asking for a coffee shop and students also need places to get community service hours, this would allow for both.

Goals and Objectives— The coffee shop would be self-sustaining. This shop would increase our students' soft skills, business skills, marketing skills, while also providing outreach opportunities. If the shop makes a profit, students will choose a charity/cause each semester to advocate for and then our proceeds would be donated to that cause.

Success Criteria— The coffee shop would be considered a success if it was self-sustaining, meaning it keeps a positive balance, and 50% of the student body visits the location.

Project Context—Staley currently has a Flock shop that serves snacks during WIN time.  Students are asking for a coffee shop. Not only that, the coffee shop will add to the overall culture of the school.  It will give students a place to hang out in the morning and possibly skip their Starbucks run.

Assumptions—Students are willing to spend money on coffee in the morning.  They are excited about new spaces to socialize with their peers.

Project Dependencies—This project depends on students being willing to volunteer, teachers being willing to help, and administration’s continued support.

Constraints—The shop cannot run all day.  Students need to be in class.  The shop will close after the 1st period.  

Risks—Students might find that the shop is inconvenient and therefore not buy coffee.  Also, there is a risk that the area won’t be supervised as well as it should be, causing behavior issues and ultimately resulting in shop closure.

Recommended Project Approach—The project will be delegated to the leadership group.  We will use a Canvas LMS class in order to organize and communicate.  This utilizes programs Staley currently has and students are familiar with.

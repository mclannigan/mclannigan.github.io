Reflection

Career Experience

I am a highly qualified high school English teacher with over 10 years of experience in the education field. Throughout my career as a teacher, I have developed strong skills in curriculum design and lesson planning as well as a deep understanding of the principles of effective instruction. I have also had the opportunity to work with various educational technologies, including learning management systems, and I have gained valuable experience in creating and delivering online content.

Furthermore, I have exercised a calculated and methodical approach to problem-solving during my teaching tenure. Not, only am I analytical. I am creative and have used my creativity to publish courses that became the model for my last school. I am independently motivated, yet I appreciate team efforts and collaborate productively within groups. Additionally, I am knowledgeable in course material development and evaluating curricula with proficiency in learning theory.

Current Educational Growth

I am currently working on my Specialist in Education though the University of Missouri, Columbia. My degree is in Learning Technologies and Design. I hope to use this degree to enhance learning and to design more effective programs.

Philosophy

As a trainer and educator, I believe that all people are able to succeed when given the tools to do so. I aim to empower learners to use technology to leverage their skills and create opportunities for growth and positive outcomes.

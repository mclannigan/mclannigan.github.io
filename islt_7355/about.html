About Me

My name is McKay Lannigan, and I am an English Teacher at North Kansas City Schools with a passion for integrating technology into my teaching. With ten years of experience, I have developed a deep love for teaching and a strong interest in utilizing technology to enhance my classroom practices. Pursuing a graduate degree in Technology is a natural step for me, as I aim to improve my skills as a twenty-first-century teacher.
I am not focused on my career, I am a proud parent of three children, aged 7 through 13. My family members each have their own unique interests and hobbies, but as a family, we all share a love of reading. My husband is an assistant principal at a local high school, and we enjoy spending as much time together as possible. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies and finding ways to integrate them into my teaching practices to engage my students and make learning more fun.

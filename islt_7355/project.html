Leadership with Ted Lasso

This mini-course invites learners to look at the great Ted Lasso and how he is an effective leader.

 Course Overview

Goal 1: Design and develop effective learning systems with the following objectives:

  • 1.1: Develop learning and performance opportunities with direct instruction, constructivist learning, collaborative work, and performance support.
  • 1.2: Create meaningful learning opportunities, engage students in online learning environments, and use appropriate technology and learning objects.
  • 1.3: Manage projects for design, development, and implementation of learning opportunities and systems.

I am able to design and develop innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable learning technologies based on theory-grounded research.

This course offers direct instruction, collaboration, support, and virtual discussions to optimize learning in a virtual environment.
